## Design Context

### Users
Content creators managing presence across Douyin, TikTok, and Red Note. They are data-literate but not engineers — they want to understand their performance without AI black boxes. Context: reviewing analytics on desktop, often comparing platforms side-by-side. The product is also shown to hackathon judges and YC-caliber investors who evaluate in 2-minute windows.

### Brand Personality
**Precise. Curious. Transparent.**

- Voice: Technical but approachable — explains the "why" behind every number
- Tone: Confident without being boastful. Data speaks, not marketing copy.
- Emotional goals: Users should feel **professional competence** ("this tool knows what it's doing") and **curiosity** ("I want to explore deeper")

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual tone**: Dense operational dashboard meets botanical dark mode. Like a trading desk designed by a garden architect.
- **Theme**: Dark mode only. Deep botanical greens (#0a0f0d base). Single accent: #7ed29a (green). Warm highlights: #f0f545 (neon pollen).
- **References**: Linear, Vercel Dashboard, Raycast — clean, precise, every pixel intentional
- **Anti-references**: Generic SaaS templates, 3-column icon-card grids, purple gradients, AI aesthetic palettes, bubbly border-radius everywhere
- **Brand**: DASH (—/) symbol + wordmark. Parent: Zonic Design Studio. Logo: horizontal dash + diagonal slash SVG (viewBox 0 0 32 20).

### Typography
- **Interface**: Geist Sans (system-ui fallback). Body 14px, headings tracking-tight.
- **Data**: Geist Mono (SF Mono fallback). All metrics, numbers, IDs use tabular-nums.
- **Kickers/Labels**: 11px, weight 500, tracking 0.04em, uppercase, muted color.
- **BANNED**: Inter, Roboto, Arial, Open Sans, serif fonts.

### Motion
- Max duration: 750ms
- Standard easing: cubic-bezier(0.22, 1, 0.36, 1)
- Animate ONLY transform + opacity (GPU-accelerated)
- Button feedback: hover translateY(-1px), active translateY(1px) scale(0.98)
- Modals: modal-enter (scale 0.97→1), backdrop fade-in with blur(12px)
- Staggered reveals for list items (100ms increments)
- prefers-reduced-motion: disable all animations, show content immediately

### Design Principles
1. **Every pixel earns its place** — No decorative elements. If it doesn't inform or enable action, remove it.
2. **Data density over whitespace** — This is an operational tool, not a marketing site. Compact, readable, information-rich.
3. **Transparency as trust** — Every score, every metric has a traceable explanation. No AI magic, no black boxes.
4. **Hierarchy through typography, not decoration** — Use font size, weight, and color to create levels. Not borders, shadows, or gradients.
5. **Subtraction default** — When in doubt, remove. The best interface is the one you don't notice.

### Anti-Patterns (BANNED)
- 3-column equal-card feature grids
- Colored left-border on cards (use corner dot instead)
- Pure black #000000 (use botanical greens)
- Emoji in UI
- Purple/cyan neon gradients
- Generic hero copy ("Welcome to...", "Unlock the power of...")
- Cards without purpose (cards must be the interaction, not decoration)
- Inline style={{ }} in new code (use Tailwind utilities + CSS variables)
